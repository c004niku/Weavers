About Us:

Description

Nekaramitra Network is a social network that provides internal and external information services related only to the handicrafts, handloom and power loom sectors.

Purpose

  • Integrating systems in the weaving sector at different locations.
  • Helping to reuse wasted resources related to the weaving sector.
  • Provide fast accurate information.
  • Collaborate to improve skills professionally.
  • Introducing traditional methods and modern technology to new generations.
  • Contribute to self-employment, career and business development.

Information Services

News : Information on events related to the weaving sector can be found here.

Alerts : Relevant information such as programs to be run by the government and other agencies.

More info : field, profession, technology, tradition, skill related information can be found.

Members: Professional information of the members can be obtained as per the registration in the application.

Shops: Members can get the details of the shops as well.

A prescribed service fee must be paid to obtain additional information services.

Sales Point: Machinery, equipment, spare parts, raw material, etc. New, rest.  Discard can be bought or put up for sale.

Forum : Local professional or business related matters Discuss open discussions with local members of the same profession.
